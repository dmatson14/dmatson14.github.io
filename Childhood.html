Childhood

  • Roosevelt was born on October 27, 1858 in Manhattan, New York City.
  • His Father, Theodore Sr. was a wealthy business man.
  • His Mother, Martha was a Southern socialite
  • He was very sick as a child and had severe asthma.
  • He ignored doctors warnings and started boxing, tennis, rowing, hiking and horseback riding.
  • He published his first book at the age of 24 and married Alice Lee
  • On Valentine's Day in 1884, both his wife and his mother passed away from diseases.
  • Distraught, he decided to move out west.
